Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It explores the concept of shared dreaming and follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who steals secrets by infiltrating the subconscious. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully plant an idea in someone's mind — a process known as inception.
